Mahatma Gandhi

“Do or die. We shall either free India or die in the attempt.”

Impacts
    Gandhi’s movements contributed to the final independence of India in August, 1947, where the British government decided to give India independence because of the constant protesting and pressure.
    He also inspired several civil rights movements in the US.
    People in the US, especially those involved in the African American rights movements, was inspired by the unique method adopted by Gandhi. This was shown by the focus of the press, which represents what people at that time favored. Also, Americans visited Gandhi and his supporters in India. When they return to the US, they spread their ideas.
    One of them was Martin Luther King, who embraced nonviolence as both the most effective and the most morally sound tactic for black deliverance, and as a way of life.15
