Motivation:
    The motivation to make the Free Officers took a stand in this situation is because their extreme dissatisfaction of the feudal control of Egypt.11
     They wanted to abolish the treaty between Egypt and British in order to stop the domination of British on Egyptian lands. And the Free Officers organization is symbolizing the leading force toward the revolution of Egypt against king Farouk.5
     They are aiming to fight against the invasion of the British troops; fight against the feudal control of king Farouk; and the Egyptians should all rule the country. Which led to the establishment of democracy that the majority wanted in Egypt.5
